Common Pricing Questions

What is a Slack team size?

It is the number of people at your company using Slack. Luno is designed to work with any size team, so we don't have any limits on your Slack team size.

When will I be billed?

We bill monthly, so you'll be charged for your plan at the end of your free trial. Your subscription will automatically renew on that day every month until you change your plan or cancel your account.

What is an FAQ Answer in Luno?

An FAQ Answer is anything you train Luno to reply with. Your team can bug Luno for FAQ Answers in Slack as often as they want.

Can I upgrade my plan?

Sure, you can change your plan at any time. You'll be charged the new monthly rate on your next scheduled payment date.

What is a Bot Trainer?

A Bot Traier is anyone who can access to your Luno Admin Dashboard to add and edit FAQ Answers.

How long is the free trial?

14 days. If you need more time, send an email to sales@lunohq.com.

What is a Slack bot?

A bot in Slack looks like a real user in your Slack team, except it can be trained with automated tasks and replies. Slack bots are a powerful way to be more productive using Slack.
