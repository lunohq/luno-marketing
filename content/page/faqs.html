Luno FAQs

How do I talk to my Lunobt?

You can DM (direct message) your Lunobot or you can add him to specific channels using /invite @luno.

Does everyone on my team need to install Luno?

No, once your Lunobot is installed, he will be available to the entire team via direct message and the channels you invite him to.

How to I invite other admins to help train my Lunobot?

Ask them go to www.lunohq.com and click the Sign In link in the upper right. When they authorize using your company's Slack account, they will automatically join your Luno account.

What default commands does my Lunobot know?

Your Lunobot is trained to reply to most hello phrases like "hi", "hello", "yo", and more.

@luno help will provide basic info to employees on what Luno is trained to answer and how to phrase requests.

@luno human will @mention the admin (you) so you can help your employee.

How much does Luno cost?

Luno is free for all teams during our beta period. Woot!
