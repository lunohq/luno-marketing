Luno FAQs

How do I talk to my Luno bot?

You can DM (direct message) your Luno bot or you can add him to specific channels using /invite @luno.

Does everyone on my team need to install Luno?

No, once your Luno bot is installed, he will be available to the entire team via direct message and the channels you invite him to.

How to I invite other people to help train my Luno bot?

You must be a Luno admin to invite others as admins or bot trainers. To send invites, log into your Luno admin dashboard and go to app.lunohq.com/admin/users.

What default commands does my Luno bot know?

Your Luno bot is trained to reply to most hello phrases like "hi", "hello", "yo", and more.

@luno help will provide basic info to employees on what Luno is trained to answer and how to phrase requests.

@luno human will @mention the admin (you) so you can help your employee.
